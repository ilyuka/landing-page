Common questions

Everything you need to know

Is this a digital product?

Yes. The toolkit is provided as editable digital files and can be used on a computer or printed for class.

What exactly is included?

Diagnostic templates, exit tickets, simple rubrics, feedback forms, peer-assessment guides and a short teacher guide.

Can I adapt the templates?

Yes. The materials are designed to be edited for different subjects, levels and classroom goals.

Is it suitable for different subjects?

Yes. The templates are general enough for language, humanities and many school subjects.

How will I receive the toolkit?

After messaging in WhatsApp, you receive product details and access instructions.

Is this for school teachers only?

It is mainly designed for school teachers, but tutors and course instructors may also adapt the materials.
